Regional focus: Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Uganda & Rwanda

Goals

100 participants will use a month to build and document a project that meets the following use cases:

  • Should contribute to social or economic growth
  • Should use Flutterwave API
  • ShoulD use Reloadly's API

Note: Other APIs can be used in building process. However the two aforementioned API are mandatory

Are You A...

  • Software Developer/Engineer?
  • Product Designer?
  • Product Manager/Architect?

The ideal team will consist of a front-end engineer, a backend engineer, product designer and a product manager.
Participants will be split into 25 teams consisting 4 members each.

Note Prosperctive participants cannot be employees of either Flutterwave or Reloadly.

How to enroll?

Participant needs to indicate their interest by registering here.

Participants will then sign up for accounts on both Flutterwave and Reloadly in order to obtain API keys and credentials which will be used in the hackathon.

For intra-team communication, participant will join a Slack channel which will be created for the sole purpose of this hackathon.

The ideal Team will consist of front-end engineer, a backend engineer, product designer and a product manager. Participants will be split into 25 Teams consisting of 4 members per team

Judgement Criteria and Process

Judges will be assigned to either Flutterwave and Reloadly. The judges will comprise of a front-end engineer, a back-end engineer, a product designer and a product manager. Judges will make use of the following criteria.

Participants work will be assessed individually after which the judges will unanimously decide on the date to round up the hackathon by sharing the result through a hosted webinar with the participants.
